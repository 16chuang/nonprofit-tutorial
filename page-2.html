(If you've already created an account, feel free to skip to the next section.)

1

Go to govoluntr.com
and click on Register.
2

Select non-profit.
3

Fill out the form, making sure to complete all of the required fields.

Your Dasbhoard

There are 3 distinct columns on your GoVo home page, or dashboard.

On the far left column, you can view your ongoing and past volunteer opportunities.

In the middle is your RAK (recent acts of kindness), an activity feed of your GoVo volunteers and non-profit friends.

On the far right is the menu bar, used for navigating around the rest of the site.

We'll go over many of the menu bar items soon.
For now, let's post our first volunteer opportunity.
