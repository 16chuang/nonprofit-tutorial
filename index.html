Tutorial Structure

Let's quickly go over the structure of this tutorial.

(If you're itching to get started, scroll to the bottom of the page.)

We'll begin the tutorial by learning how to find your way around your new account and the GoVo site.

Then we'll dive straight into the volunteer opportunity posting process from start to finish.

Finally, we'll end with an overview of the other exciting features GoVoluntr gives you access to.

Pages

Each webpage in this tutorial presents a distinct and coherent set of topics. You can track your progress through pages on the top floating menu. You can advance through these pages via the menu, or by just following the links at the bottom of each page.

Sections

Each individual webpage is further divided into sections. You can track your progress through these sections on the right side of the page. You can access these sections by simply scrolling through the page.
